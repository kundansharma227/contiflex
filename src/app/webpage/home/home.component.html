About Company

Leveraging on our extensive industry experience of 34 years, we "Continental Commercial Company", established in the year 1977 have placed ourselves as one of the trusted manufacturers, suppliers, retailers, wholesalers and exporters of Machine Lamp, Coolant Pipe, Collets & Other Industrial Products. All our products have high utility in varied industries such as automobile, shipping, jewelery and others. Our range comprises Flexible Shaft, Grinder Machine, Extension Lamp, Spot Light, Press Light and Arm Light. In addition, we offer Tier Lamp, PL CNC Lamp, Lamps, CNC Machines Fergoflex Coolant Pipe, Spout Assembly and Flex-o-Line PVC Coolant Pipe. The base of our lamp is durable, strong and easy to fix on the wall or on a machine. These lamps can move forward or backward, left to right and up or down. Our lamps find an extensive application in automobile, textile, pharmaceuticals, engineering, jewelery, packaging, electrical and a host of other industries. Clients can avail these products that are known for their scratch resistant, durable and energy efficient attribute. For the production of a superior quality product, we have equipped sophisticated machines at our infrastructure. These high-tech machinery has allowed us to develop a world-class array of products in a short frame of time. With the help of these machines, we have been able to fulfill even the bulk requirements of the clients. For the proper maintenance and operation of these high-tech machines, we have appointed the most proficient technicians. Further, we have a R&D unit that is responsible for improvisation of technology. Owing to all these factors, we have been able to gain a huge clientele across the nation.
